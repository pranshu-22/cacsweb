About CCMT/CCMN

CCMT is a common platform for candidates to apply for M.Tech./ M.Arch./ M.Plan./M.Des. programmes, based on their GATE score of previous three years, in all NITs, IIEST Shibpur, and some IIITs & GFTIs. For details, please visit the official website ccmt.nic.in.
Similarly, CCMN is a common platform for candidates to apply for M.Sc./ M.Sc. (Tech.) programmes based on their JAM score of the current year, in NITs and GFTIs. This year 20 NITs, IIEST Shibpur, and SLIET Longowal are participating in the CCMN counselling process.

Both, CCMT & CCMN are being organized by Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur in 2021.

Due to pandemic situation, Document Verification shall be done online. For this purpose, after seat allotment, the candidates will be required to upload the needful documents. For all guidelines, the prospective candidates are advised to carefully read the information brochure and various other documents given on the official websites of CCMT-2021 & CCMN-2021.
