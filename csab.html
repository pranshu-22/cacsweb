About CSAB

To apply for admission to B.Tech./ B.Arch./ B.Plan. programmes in NITs, IIEST Shibpur and some IIITs & GFTIs (commonly known as NIT+ system), a candidate must register in JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority), a common platform for admission in NIT+ system and IITs. For details, please visit the official website of JoSAA josaa.nic.in.
After completion of main rounds of JoSAA, the vacant seats of NIT+ system are filled by CSAB (Central Seat Allocation Board). For details, please visit the official website of CSAB csab.nic.in.
CSAB is being organized by Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur in 2021. Due to pandemic situation, Document Verification shall be done online. For this purpose, after seat allotment, the candidates will be required to upload the needful documents. For all guidelines, the prospective candidates are advised to carefully read the information brochure and various other documents given on the official websites of JoSAA-2021 & CSAB-2021.
