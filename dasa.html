About DASA

Direct Admission of Students Abroad (DASA) scheme of Ministry of Education, Government of India provides an excellent opportunity to the Foreign Nationals / Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) / Non- Resident Indians (NRI) / Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) to study in various Undergraduate and Postgraduate programmes in premier Indian technical institutions (National Institutes of Technology (NITs)/Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST)/ Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs)/ Schools of Planning and Architecture (SPAs)/ and various other centrally funded institutions).
The UG admissions for academic year 2021-22 under DASA scheme shall be on the basis of the ranks obtained by the students in JEE (Main). For details, please visit the official website of DASA at www.dasanit.org .

About

Direct Admission of Students Abroad (DASA) is conducted for admission to the various Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) programmes of the leading institutes of India under DASA scheme. Candidates will get admission under the DASA scheme in coordination with Malaviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur, India for the year 2021-22. The admission will be based on the JEE Main rank.
